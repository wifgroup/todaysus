# Today’s US — Project Overview

## Overview
**Today’s US** is an independent digital news publication focused on clear, factual reporting about the United States.  
The platform is designed to provide context-driven journalism across U.S. news, politics, business, technology, and society.

Rather than chasing speed or clickbait, Today’s US prioritizes:
- Accuracy
- Verification
- Editorial clarity
- Reader trust

The project is currently in its **pre-launch / soft-launch phase**, with a fully developed newsroom-style website structure and publishing standards in place.

---

## Mission
To help readers understand **what is happening in the United States and why it matters**, through responsible reporting, analysis, and explainers.

Today’s US aims to reduce noise in the modern news ecosystem by focusing on:
- Facts over speculation  
- Context over headlines  
- Transparency over virality  

---

## Editorial Focus
Today’s US covers topics that directly shape public life in the United States.

### Core Coverage Areas
- **U.S. News** — National reporting and major developments
- **Politics** — Government, elections, policy, and institutions
- **Business & Economy** — Markets, companies, labor, and economic policy
- **Technology** — Artificial intelligence, digital policy, cybersecurity, innovation
- **World** — Global events with direct impact on the U.S.
- **Opinion** — Clearly labeled commentary grounded in facts

Each section operates as a distinct editorial desk, following consistent standards.

---

## Editorial Standards
Today’s US is built on established newsroom principles.

### Key Standards
- Editorial independence from political parties, corporations, and advocacy groups
- Clear separation between news, analysis, and opinion
- Fact-checking before publication
- Transparent corrections when errors occur
- Disclosure of conflicts of interest
- Responsible use of AI tools (as assistance, not as sole authorship)

A dedicated **Editorial Policy** and **Corrections Policy** are part of the site structure.

---

## Website Structure
The platform follows a **publisher-grade architecture** aligned with Google News and search engine guidelines.

### Core Pages
- Homepage
- Category pages (News, Politics, Business, Technology, World, Opinion)
- Individual article pages
- Author profile pages

### Trust & Compliance Pages
- About Us
- Contact
- Editorial Policy
- Corrections
- Ethics
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use

This structure ensures transparency, discoverability, and long-term scalability.

## Technology & Design Philosophy
The website is built with:
- Clean, semantic HTML
- Modern CSS with theme variables for scalability
- Lightweight JavaScript for accessibility and performance
- Mobile-first, responsive design
- Accessibility and reduced-motion support
- SEO- and Google News–friendly markup and schema

The design emphasizes:
- Readability
- Editorial hierarchy
- Minimalism
- Long-term maintainability
